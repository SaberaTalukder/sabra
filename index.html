About Me

First off thanks for visiting my personal page! My name is Sabera Talukder and I am currently a senior at Stanford University, and will be graduating this spring (2018) with a double major with honors in both in electrical engineering (track: control systems), and in biology (track: biochemistry). During my time at Stanford I worked on two main projects: building a portable electrostatic precipitator to reduce respiratory deaths in rural environments which was published at 2017 IEEE Compel, and decipering how no-net motion visual memories can be entrained and instantiated in fruit flies. Visit my project page to learn about those two and more!

Other than work I love to travel, dance salsa, run, do any water sport, and eat amazing foods. Throughout my site you'll see photos of me doing just that! I hope you have a great time getting to know me in an albeit limited capacity, and please reach out to me if there is anything you need or that you know might be of interest to me at sabera@stanford.edu.
