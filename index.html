Welcome!


My name is Sabera Talukder and I build time series foundation models and novel multimodal representations! I am a National Science Foundation, Chen, and PIMCO Graduate Fellow as well as a Caltech PhD candidate working with Professors Georgia Gkioxari and Yisong Yue (primary advisor).

Previously, I served as a Chair on the COSYNE Oraganizing Committee, scaled time series foundation models as a machine learning research intern at Google Research, and created interdisciplinary ML + Neuro projects between Stanford, Berkeley & UCSF as a software engineer at the Chan Zuckerberg Biohub. Before that, I double majored at Stanford with honors in both electrical engineering and biochemistry.

I've led many past lives from being a competitive synchronized swimmer (National Gold Medal, National Silver Medal, California State Games Gold Medal) to designing low-cost water and air purification systems and even deploying the former in Bangladesh (Google Science Fair Finalist, Scientific American Science in Action Finalist).

Feel free to reach out if you think we should connect!
