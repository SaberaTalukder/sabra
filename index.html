Welcome!



My name is Sabera Talukder and I am a National Science Foundation Graduate Fellow, Chen Graduate Fellow, and PhD candidate at Caltech working with Professor Yisong Yue. My research is in the burgeoning field of neuroengineering, where I combine deep learning and invasive, human, neural data to create models that generalize across patients. Previously, I was a software engineer at the Chan Zuckerberg Biohub where I led the neuroengineering efforts. Before that, I graduated from Stanford with a double major with honors in both electrical engineering and biochemistry. Feel free to reach out if you think we should connect!
